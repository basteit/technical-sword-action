# Hotfix Notes (2026-05-08)

## Fixed
1. Player now flips left/right with move input.
2. Dash now ignores incoming hits while active.
3. Hit reaction strengthened (slightly longer hit-lock + minimum knockback).
4. Contact push reduced: enemies move on hit knockback, not by simple body contact.
5. Continuous contact damage works again via trigger stay + repeat interval.
6. During player invincibility, collisions with selected enemy layers are temporarily ignored.

## Inspector Check (important)
- `PlayerDamageReceiver2D.ignoreCollisionLayersWhileInvincible`
  - Include `Enemy` (and any enemy hurtbox layer).
- `DamageSource2D.repeatInterval`
  - Recommended start: `0.15`.
- Enemy `Damageable2D.blockContactPush`
  - Keep ON.
- Player `PlayerMotor2D.flipSpriteByMoveInput`
  - Keep ON.

## Quick Validation
1. Move left then attack: attack should face left.
2. Dash through enemy hitbox: no damage during dash window.
3. Stand inside enemy trigger: repeated damage should occur over time (respecting invincibility).
4. Touch enemy body without attack: enemy should not be pushed around.
5. On hit, player knockback should feel clearer than before.
